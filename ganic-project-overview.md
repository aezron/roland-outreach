# GANIC

GANIC is an AI-assisted drum sequencer project focused on creative rhythm generation and musician-friendly experimentation.

Live project:
https://ganic.ronde.tools/

## My Contribution

My contribution to GANIC was focused on the user-facing side of the project rather than model development.

I worked on:
- graphic design
- visual presentation
- product input
- testing and user-perspective feedback

This gave me practical exposure to how AI-driven music ideas need to be shaped into workflows that are clear, usable, and creatively meaningful for musicians.

## Why It Matters To Me

My background spans software development, music technology, and live sound engineering. That made GANIC especially interesting to me because it sits at the intersection of creative tooling, musician workflow, and applied AI.
